জিজ্ঞাসাবাদ (FAQ)

Frequently Asked Questions

Find details regarding the daily mess menu, security deposits, curfew rules, and guest policies.

Our monthly rent covers accommodation (wooden beds, study tables, marble flooring), 3 daily home-style meals from our on-site mess, high-speed Wi-Fi, daily sanitization of shared washrooms, and gated security access. There are zero hidden extra charges.

We require a 1-month security deposit along with the running month’s rent in advance during admission. The deposit is fully refundable upon giving a 1-month written notice prior to vacating.

To guarantee the safety of all boarders, our main security gate is locked at 10:30 PM daily. Special extensions for study groups, examinations, or night shifts are allowed with prior notification to the warden.

Our mess is highly rated in Saratpally for consistent quality. We serve fresh, hot Bengali home meals cooked daily. Breakfast, lunch (rice, dal, sabji, fish/egg/chicken), and dinner are prepared using hygienic ingredients.

Parents and family members are welcome to visit boarders in our common visitor lounges. Overnight guest stays are subject to prior management approval and room availability.
